Poletto's Restaurant

2021

Arzu Kartal Studio. Interior design

Arzu Kartal has been working as a self-employed interior designer in Hamburg for the past eight years, predominantly designing luxurious spaces by using well-known furniture brands and high-end materials. She specializes in residential and commercial design, but also designs offices. When I worked with Arzu, I managed my own projects, took care of the full design concept and set up new management tools, such as an agenda for the workers, a bill of quantities linked to a design booklet, project details for moodboards and layouts.
